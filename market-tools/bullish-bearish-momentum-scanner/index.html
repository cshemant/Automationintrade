How to use this tool

Quickly separate strong momentum from weak momentum

Bullish momentum highlights stocks with positive daily movement and high strength score. Bearish momentum highlights stocks with negative movement and weaker score. Reversal and pullback watchlists help you avoid looking at only one side of the market.
