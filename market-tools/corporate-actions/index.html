How to use this tool

Do not miss ex-date and record-date events

Use this tracker to quickly identify stocks with corporate actions. Dividends affect ex-date planning, bonus and split events adjust price and quantity, buybacks need entitlement checks, and rights issues need careful date and pricing verification.

DividendCheck ex-date, record date and actual yield. Price usually adjusts around ex-date.
Bonus / SplitQuantity changes, price adjusts, and market value does not increase automatically.
Buyback / RightsVerify route, eligibility, ratio, price and application timeline before acting.
