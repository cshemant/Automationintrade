About Automation In Trade

We simplify stock market data into clean, actionable visual insights.

Automation In Trade is a stock market data automation platform that converts complex market data into clean tools, charts, dashboards, and social-media-ready visuals for faster analysis.

Explore Tools View AIT Indicator

Market mood, result pulse, index signals and stock tools in one visual ecosystem.

Why this platform exists

Manual market analysis is slow when data is scattered.

Most traders spend too much time reading scattered data from results, charts, option chains, news, institutional holdings and sector movement. Automation In Trade simplifies this process by converting raw market data into ready-to-understand dashboards, cards, and visual reports.

Our focus

Data clarity first. Blind tips never.

Our focus is not to give blind buy/sell calls. Our focus is to simplify market data, highlight risk zones, show market mood, and help users make better-informed decisions using structured data.

What we build

Automation tools for results, price action, option data and market flow.

Each tool is designed to convert a specific market dataset into a quick, visual and decision-friendly output.
