SEO overview

Track sector rotation and institutional money flow in seconds.

Sector Money Flow helps traders and investors identify sector rotation by showing institutional inflow and outflow across sectors. It is useful for swing traders, positional traders and investors who want to focus on sectors where money is entering and avoid sectors where money is consistently leaving.

IN

Sector Buying Flow

Shows sectors receiving positive institutional flow, helping you quickly identify where market support is building.

OUT

Sector Selling Flow

Highlights sectors where money is moving out, useful for avoiding weak rotation or reducing exposure.

AUC

Current Allocation

Displays current sector exposure, helping you understand where institutional allocation is already concentrated.

Trader use case

How this helps swing and positional traders.

Instead of checking many sectors manually, the tool summarizes sector-level activity into ready-to-read visuals. A swing trader can shortlist stocks from sectors seeing inflow, while a positional trader can track whether institutions are supporting a sector over a longer period.

It also helps avoid weak pockets: if a sector shows strong outflow, the trader can be more cautious with fresh entries, reduce watchlist noise, or wait for confirmation before taking a position.
