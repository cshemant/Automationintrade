Option Sentiment

Live option-chain mood → score, bias, levels and visual cards.

Option Sentiment retrieves live, minute-by-minute option-chain data for your watchlist or custom stock list. It helps you compare hundreds—even thousands—of stocks during live market hours and quickly understand option-chain mood using Excel summaries and clean visual cards.

What it does

Analyze option-chain pressure without manually decoding every strike.

The tool reads live option-chain data and converts key fields such as PCR, writer bias, trend, max pain, IV skew, volume/OI, ATM delta and combined sentiment into a simple scorecard. You can inspect the Excel sheet for deeper analysis or use the visuals to decide within seconds.

PCR

PCR + Writer Bias

Highlights whether call or put activity is dominating and whether writers are building bullish, bearish or neutral pressure.

MP

Max Pain + Range

Shows max pain placement, projected range, support, resistance and important CE/PE wall zones.

IV

IV Skew + Vol/OI

Tracks skew, volume/OI and ATM Greeks to identify pressure, caution zones and possible sentiment shifts.

Excel intelligence layer

Use the Excel sheet for deep comparison across many stocks.

The Excel output acts like a live market dashboard. It lets you compare CMP, PCR, trend, CE/PE strikes, predicted high/low, support, resistance, IV skew, max pain, Greeks, volume, premiums and strike-level activity across your full watchlist.

If you do not want to read the sheet during market hours, the visual cards compress the same idea into a quick final verdict, confidence score and action-plan view.
